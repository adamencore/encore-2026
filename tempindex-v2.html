All Audiences Welcome

Theatre that meets every family where they are.

Live performance should be open to every audience. Select performances throughout the season are designed with accessibility at the center.

Sensory-Friendly Performances

A relaxed, welcoming environment for audience members with sensory sensitivities, autism, or anyone who experiences theatre best with a little more freedom.

  • Softened lighting & lowered sound levels
  • House lights kept slightly raised
  • Quiet space available throughout the show
  • Freedom to move, talk, or step out as needed
  • Pre-show social story sent to all families
See sensory-friendly schedule

ASL-Interpreted Performances

Certified ASL interpreters bring the full performance to life for our Deaf and hard-of-hearing audience members at select dates each season.

  • Two interpreters per performance (character + ensemble)
  • Reserved sightline seating at no extra cost
  • Pre-show meet-and-greet with interpreters
  • Scripts available in advance upon request
  • Offered for all Signature & Show with a Pro productions
See ASL performance dates

Our Story So Far

What five years of young artists can build.

What began in 2021 as a small community of families who believed Southern Utah's young performers deserved a stage of their own has grown into a year-round home for hundreds of artists each season.

Every production, every camp, every show choir rehearsal has been built one moment at a time — by students, mentors, parents, and a community that keeps showing up.

Make It Possible

Every young artist on our stage stands on the shoulders of supporters like you.

Ticket sales cover only a fraction of what it takes to put on a season. Costumes, sets, scholarships, and the staff who mentor our young artists are made possible by the generosity of families, businesses, and friends who believe in this work.
